About Us

Boston Film Festival

Hi! I am Clara Dunn, the founder of Watch it outside events Co. and I am organizing an outdoor festival in Boston this year called 'Watch it outside Boston edition'.

I hope to select and show films from August 5th to 8th in Boston Public Garden, a park in Boston that can hold up to 1,500 people.

My company, "Watch it Outside" just got the approval to show movies in Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. We can't wait to help the public discover awesome films!
